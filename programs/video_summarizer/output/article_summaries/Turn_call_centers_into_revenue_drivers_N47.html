Summary

Turn Call Centers Into Revenue Drivers: AI Transformation with Observe AI

In this comprehensive interview, Swapnil Jain, founder and CEO of Observe AI, discusses how artificial intelligence is fundamentally transforming contact centers from cost centers into revenue drivers. Founded in 2017 as a "pre-LLM company," Observe AI has grown to serve 300+ enterprise customers including major banks and telecom companies.

The AI Contact Center Revolution

Observe AI provides three core products that represent the future of customer service:

  • AI Agents: Autonomous voice and chat agents that handle simple, repetitive customer queries
  • Real-time AI Co-pilots: AI assistants that listen to live calls and guide human agents with real-time suggestions and insights
  • Post-interaction Analytics: AI-powered analysis of all customer interactions to improve quality, compliance, and business outcomes

Industry Transformation Predictions

Jain predicts that 50% or more of customer service calls will be automated by AI within the next five years. However, he envisions a hybrid future where AI handles routine queries while human agents focus on complex, sensitive, or empathy-requiring interactions.

The BPO Industry Disruption

The Business Process Outsourcing (BPO) industry faces significant challenges as AI automation reduces the need for human agents. Jain argues that traditional "service-as-software" transformation won't work for contact centers because enterprises maintain strict control over customer service processes, unlike other outsourced functions like legal services.

From Cost Center to Revenue Driver

A key insight is how AI enables contact centers to transform from cost centers into revenue generators. By automating simple queries with AI agents, companies can:

  • Reduce operational costs significantly
  • Redeploy human agents for cross-selling and upselling
  • Increase customer touchpoints without proportional cost increases
  • Improve customer experience while driving business growth

Technology and Innovation Approach

Observe AI follows a "sell then build" philosophy, using customer feedback as a forcing function for rapid product development. The company combines foundation models (OpenAI, Anthropic) with proprietary small language models (SLMs) and domain-specific AI workflows. Their testing framework even uses AI agents to test other AI agents, creating a meta-AI development approach.

Global Operations and Leadership

With 200+ employees in India and significant US operations, Observe AI demonstrates how to build customer-centric products across time zones. Jain emphasizes the importance of continuous CEO evolution, becoming more intense and ambitious as the company scales, driven by the realization that in venture-backed companies, there are only two outcomes: massive success or failure.
