Turn Call Centers Into Revenue Drivers: AI Transformation with ObserveAI

This comprehensive interview with Swapnil Jain, founder and CEO of ObserveAI, explores how artificial intelligence is fundamentally transforming contact centers from cost centers into revenue-driving business units. Founded in 2017 as a pre-LLM company, ObserveAI has grown to serve 300+ enterprise customers including major banks and telecom companies.

The AI Revolution in Contact Centers

ObserveAI operates as a contact center AI platform helping large enterprises leverage AI across three key areas: automated AI agents for simple calls, real-time AI co-pilots for human agents, and post-interaction analytics for performance optimization. The company's philosophy centers on a hybrid approach where AI handles routine queries while humans manage complex, sensitive interactions requiring empathy.

Founder's Journey and Vision

Jain's path to founding ObserveAI began during his time at Twitter (2012-2015), followed by 18 months in India exploring various business ideas including insurance and credit cards. His aha moment came from spending six months in Manila's call centers, observing agents' daily challenges and recognizing the need for AI-first software solutions. This customer-problem-first approach became central to the company's DNA.

Industry Transformation Predictions

Jain predicts that 50%+ of customer service calls will be automated by AI within five years, though he distinguishes between Silicon Valley hype and enterprise reality. Many large companies still operate legacy on-premise systems requiring years of infrastructure updates before implementing AI solutions. The segmentation focuses on knowledge-based queries (easily automated) versus workflow-based queries requiring complex integrations.

BPO Industry Impact

Traditional BPOs face significant disruption as their primary business model of selling human agents becomes obsolete. Unlike other service industries that can transform through software-as-a-service models, contact center outsourcing remains constrained by enterprise-dictated processes, technology stacks, and operational requirements. This prevents BPOs from independently innovating or improving margins through AI adoption.

Product Portfolio Strategy

ObserveAI's three-product strategy addresses the complete AI transformation journey: Voice AI Agents automate simple interactions, Real-time AI Co-pilots enhance human agent performance through live guidance and transcription, and Post-interaction Analytics provide insights for continuous improvement. All products utilize both proprietary small language models and partnerships with major LLM providers.

The Innovator's Dilemma Response

Facing the classic challenge of existing revenue streams being disrupted by new technology, ObserveAI chose self-disruption over waiting to be disrupted. The company actively cannibalizes its human-centric products by offering customers the ability to convert existing seat-based spending into AI agent investments, maintaining revenue while future-proofing the business model.

Revenue Center Transformation

A key insight emerges around contact centers evolving from cost centers to revenue drivers. As AI reduces costs for simple interactions, businesses can strategically deploy human agents for cross-selling, upselling, and acquisition activities. This aligns with the broader digitization trend where virtual business interactions increase the need for sophisticated customer service capabilities.

Global Operations and Culture

With 200+ employees in India and leadership in the Bay Area, ObserveAI demonstrates how distributed teams can maintain customer centricity through structured processes: quarterly customer visits, regular win-story sharing, and engineering team participation in customer meetings. The company leverages India's competitive AI talent market while managing time zone challenges.
