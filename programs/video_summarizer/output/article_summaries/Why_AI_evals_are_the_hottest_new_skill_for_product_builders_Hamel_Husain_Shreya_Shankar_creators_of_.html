Why AI Evals Are the Hottest New Skill for Product Builders

🎯 The Rise of Evals in AI Product Development ⏰ 0:00

AI evaluations (evals) have emerged as the most critical skill for modern product builders working with AI applications. As emphasized by chief product officers at both Anthropic and OpenAI, evals represent a fundamental shift in how we measure and improve AI product performance. This podcast episode features Hamel Husain and Shreya Shankar, creators of the #1 Maven course on evals, who have taught over 2,000 PMs and engineers across 500+ companies.

🔍 What Are Evals? ⏰ 5:51

At its core, an eval is "data analytics on your LLM application in a systematic way" that allows teams to measure performance and iterate improvements. Rather than being a scary or unapproachable concept, evals provide a structured framework for understanding where AI applications succeed and fail, enabling targeted enhancements.

⚡ Core Philosophy: Progress Over Perfection ⏰ 0:18

The fundamental principle of effective evals is that "the goal is not to do evals perfectly. It's to actionably improve your product." This mindset shift helps teams focus on practical improvements rather than getting bogged down in theoretical perfection. The highest ROI activity for AI product development is building robust evaluation systems that provide clear, actionable insights.

🚫 Common Misconceptions and Pitfalls ⏰ 0:36

One of the biggest misconceptions is the belief that "Can't the AI just eval it?" However, as the experts explain, this approach simply doesn't work effectively. Many teams have been burned by poorly implemented evals in the past, leading to anti-eval sentiment. The key is implementing the "benevolent dictator" approach - appointing one trusted person with domain expertise (often the product manager) to guide the eval process rather than getting bogged down in committee decisions.

📊 The Manual Analysis Foundation ⏰ 1:12

Effective eval development starts with manual error analysis of real user interactions. This process involves examining actual user traces, identifying patterns in failures, and reaching "theoretical saturation" in error identification. This human-guided analysis forms the foundation for developing automated evaluation systems that actually reflect real-world performance issues.

🎯 Implementation Strategy ⏰ 10:00

The most successful eval implementations follow a systematic approach: start with manual analysis, categorize error types, develop targeted evaluation criteria, and then build both code-based and LLM-as-judge evaluations. Teams typically spend about 30 minutes per week maintaining evals after the initial setup, making this a highly sustainable practice for continuous improvement.

🏆 Business Impact and Industry Adoption

Companies that excel at evals are becoming some of the fastest-growing in AI, with entire businesses built around providing evaluation services to AI labs. The skill has become so essential that evals are now considered "the new PRDs" - fundamental documents that guide AI product development and improvement strategies.
